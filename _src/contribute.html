We are experimenting with news content and open publishing practices. Join us if you value our way of doing, and want to take part to this.

Contributing is simple:

  • If you are a blogger, journalist, or someone doing/building stuff, send us inspiring stories to feature.
  • If you are a designer or developer, help us with building the site's features. Get in touch to propose your idea, or send pull requests on github.com.

Once your first contribution is accepted, you become a member of the team.
