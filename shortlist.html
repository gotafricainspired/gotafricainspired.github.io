Sénamé Agbodjinou: Archicamps, fablabs, and the african smart city idea

A talk (in french) at Lift in Marseille by Sénamé Agbodjinou of L'Africaine d'Architecture and WoeLab. He presents what they have been doing in Togo with the Archicamps idea, Fablabs, and the dream of an african smart city.

Jason Njoku: Failing all the way to Success

Jason Njoku is a Nigerian Internet entrepreneur, and is CEO and founder of Iroko Partners, the creators of IrokoTV.com. In this talk at TEDxEuston 2011, he gives his view on Success, based on his own story and the failures that are part of the entrepreneur's journey.

  • 09 Feb 2014
