An exhibition of this stunning work is on display now in Sydney at Pine Street Creative Arts Centre in Pine Street, Chippendale.

How I got to know a bit more about Jason Njoku, and truely got inspired

Jason Njoku is a Nigerian Internet entrepreneur, and is CEO and founder of Iroko Partners, the creators of IrokoTV.com.
In this talk at TEDxEuston 2011, he gives his view on Success, based on his own story and the failures that are part of the entrepreneur's journey. And he keeps repeating something that resonates in me: The importance of embracing failure.
Fear not! Just get going!
